India Visa Services

Your guide to seamless travel to India

Visa Types

  • Tourist Visa: For leisure and tourism purposes.
  • Business Visa: For business meetings, conferences, or related activities.
  • Student Visa: For pursuing education in India.
  • Medical Visa: For medical treatments and accompanying caretakers.

Required Documents

  • Passport with at least 6 months validity and two blank pages.
  • Recent passport-sized photograph (as per specifications).
  • Completed visa application form.
  • Proof of return or onward travel (e.g., flight tickets).
  • Hotel booking or address of stay in India.
  • Business/Invitation letters for Business or Student Visa.

Application Process

Step 1

Fill the online visa application form on the official portal.

Step 2

Upload required documents and pay the visa fee.

Step 3

Schedule an appointment at the nearest Indian Embassy or Consulate.
